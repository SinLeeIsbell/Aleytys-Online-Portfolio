A Little Bit Personal

Why "Persistent and Unafraid"?

Growing up, I'd hear my mom often say, "You're your father's child. Stubborn." I wore it as a badge of pride as a child. But as I grew up, "stubborn" wasn't the right quality to keep. Instead, I took what was good about stubborn and turned that into "tenacity".

Before it all happened, I never imagined working in Japan, holding an officer position in Toastmasters, living in Germany, becoming a paralegal, or deciding on a career in tech. Once I want something to work out, I work hard for it. I get creative, I communicate, I research and I practice, practice, practice.
